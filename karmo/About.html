ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Evan, a first year architecture student at Carnegie Mellon University.

I am from Watertown, Connecticut, but was actually born in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I was adopted when I was three months old, and moved to the United States. Currently, I live with my parents, brother (who was also adopted from Vietnam), and my dog and cat. My dad is a faculty member at The Taft School while my mother is a physical therapist and high school teacher in Waterbury, CT. After graduating from Taft, I decided to follow my passion for architecture to Pittsburgh. Outside of Architecture, my interests include Sports, Environmental Studies, Travel, and Cryptozoology. At Carnegie Mellon, I am on the Club Baseball team, Astronomy Club, and am an active member of the Sigma Chi Fraternity.
